How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your BPT Academy account and associated data at any time by following these steps:

  1. Send an email to office@bptacademy.uk with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the full name and email address associated with your BPT Academy account
  3. We will verify your identity and confirm receipt within 2 business days
  4. Your account and data will be deleted within 30 days of your verified request

What Data Is Deleted

Upon a confirmed deletion request, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Your account profile (name, date of birth, phone number, profile photo)
  • Training progress, attendance records, and coaching notes
  • In-app messages and conversations
  • Notifications and announcements
  • Tournament registrations and rankings
  • Push notification tokens and device data

What Data Is Retained

Some data may be retained after account deletion where required by law or legitimate business purposes:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Payment records 7 years from transaction date UK tax law (HMRC requirement)
Anonymised usage data Indefinitely (no personal identifiers) Service improvement and analytics

Parent / Guardian Requests (Junior Accounts)

If you are requesting deletion of a child's account, please include the child's name and confirm your relationship as parent or guardian in your email. We will verify parental authority before processing the request.

Questions?

If you have any questions about your data or this process, contact us at office@bptacademy.uk.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or call 0303 123 1113.
