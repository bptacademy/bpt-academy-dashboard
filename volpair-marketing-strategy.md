## 1. Brand Positioning

### Core Message
volpair is not a dating app. It's where padel players find their people — a playing partner, a community, a night out, and sometimes something more.

The dating angle is real, but it's one outcome of a social platform built around a shared obsession. This is the key differentiation: **Tinder is for anyone. volpair is for your tribe.**

### Taglines (options — choose one for launch)
- **"Every rally starts with a match."** ← primary recommendation (already in app)
- "Find your pair on and off the court."
- "Your padel circle, upgraded."
- "The court has a new match system."

### Why "Every Rally Starts with a Match" wins
It works on multiple levels: a padel rally, a romantic match, finding a playing partner. It's active, sport-specific, and memorable. No other dating or social app can say this.

### Positioning Matrix
| | Generic Dating Apps | Generic Sports Apps | **volpair** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Padel-specific | ✗ | Sometimes | ✅ |
| Social + dating + community | Dating only | Social only | ✅ All three |
| court history data | ✗ | ✗ | ✅ volpair Score |
| Club events / party listings | ✗ | ✗ | ✅ |
| UK padel focus | ✗ | Generic | ✅ |

### Three Target Personas

**Persona 1 — The Social Player ("Alex", 28)**
Plays 2–3x/week. Uses Playtomic or OTC to book courts. Active on Instagram. Goes to club social nights. Not actively dating but open to meeting someone through padel. Primary motivation: find a regular doubles partner and stay connected to the scene.
→ Hook: Radar + Find a Partner

**Persona 2 — The Single Looking ("Sofia", 31)**
Recently got into padel (6–18 months). Loves the social side. Tired of generic dating apps — wants to meet someone with a shared interest. Slightly aspirational lifestyle — works hard, plays hard.
→ Hook: Connect + volpair Score

**Persona 3 — The Club Regular ("Marcus", 35)**
Has a strong court circle already. Follows padel accounts, attends tournaments. Might convert to premium for advanced features. Could become a community ambassador.
→ Hook: volpair Score + Club Party Listings (as attendee)

## 4. Growth Phase — Year 1 (Oct 2026–Aug 2027)

### Monthly Content Rhythm
Each month needs content across these 4 pillars:

**Pillar 1: Court Life** — real moments, real players, the padel lifestyle
*"The game we play, the people we meet"*
→ Why: aspirational but accessible. Shows the world they belong to.

**Pillar 2: Product Features** — Radar, volpair Score, Connect explained in action
*Show don't tell — screen recordings, demos, "did you know?"*
→ Why: drives downloads and feature adoption

**Pillar 3: Match Stories** — anonymous or named pairs who met on volpair
*Playing partners, doubles partners, couples — all count*
→ Why: social proof is the most powerful conversion driver for social/dating apps

**Pillar 4: Club & Community** — events, party nights, club features
*"Find out what's happening at [Club] this weekend"*
→ Why: positions volpair as the hub of the UK padel social scene

### Retention Tactics
- **Weekly push notifications:** "3 players are on radar near you right now"
- **Post-match prompts:** 24h after court session — rate your partners, view prompts
- **volpair Score updates:** "Your score changed — check your new matches"
- **Club events digest:** "This weekend near you on volpair" (pushes premium + club listings)
- **Monthly milestone emails:** "This month you connected with X players"

## 5. OTC Scenario — With Partnership

### How OTC Changes Everything
OTC is a booking platform with an existing user base of UK padel players — the exact target market. A partnership means:
- volpair gets introduced to OTC's club network (600+ UK clubs) from day one
- Co-branded content reaches OTC users at the point of highest engagement (just booked a court)
- Club party listings become easier to sell — OTC can bundle the introduction
- Revised Year 1 target: **5,000–10,000 users** (vs 3,000 conservative without OTC)

### What to Ask OTC For
1. **In-app integration** — "Download volpair" prompt after a booking confirmation
2. **Club communications** — joint email to OTC club partners introducing volpair
3. **Co-branded content** — 2x joint Instagram posts/month
4. **Data sharing** — aggregate (anonymised) match history for volpair Score (subject to legal review)
5. **Event co-hosting** — joint launch event at Big Box Padel Club (home of BPT Academy), co-promoted to OTC's club network

### What OTC Gets
- 20% revenue share on subscription revenue (as agreed)
- "Powered by On The Court" branding on volpair profiles of OTC-connected users
- Direct product input — founding partner status
- Increased engagement / court bookings driven by volpair's "Find a Partner" feature

### OTC Meeting Deck — Key Elements
The OTC deck needs to include (separate deck, not this document):

## 7. Influencer & Affiliate Strategy

### Identification Criteria (UK Padel Influencers)
Search: Instagram + TikTok for #padeluk #padellovers #padellife #britishpadel
Filter: UK-based, genuine padel content (not just gym/lifestyle), engagement rate >3%

### Tier Structure

**Tier 1 — Paid Partners (10k+ followers)**
- Fee: £100–300/post
- Deliverable: 1 Reel + 3 Stories per month
- What they get: Fee + free premium subscription
- Budget: £300–500/mo (1–2 Tier 1 per month)
- Source: Via Eutopia Agency (Aimee's agency) — huge advantage

**Tier 2 — Affiliate Micro (2k–10k followers)**
- Fee: Free premium subscription + £0.50 per new signup via their link
- Deliverable: 1 post/month + referral code in bio
- What they get: Free premium + commission
- Budget: ~£50–100/mo (depends on conversions)
- Target: 10–20 micro influencers by Month 3

**Tier 3 — Community Ambassadors (<2k followers)**
- Fee: Free premium subscription
- Deliverable: Organic posts when they feel like it, share referral code
- What they get: Free premium + early access + community status
- Budget: £0
- Target: 20–50 ambassadors — coaches, club regulars, BPT Academy students

### Outreach Message Template
> Hi [Name], I'm Aimee — co-founder of volpair, the new padel social app launching in the UK this September. I've been following your content and love how you capture the community side of padel.
>
> We're building a small group of padel personalities to be part of the launch — I'd love to offer you [free premium / a fee] to share your experience with your audience.
>
> Would you be open to a quick chat? Happy to tell you more about what we're building.
>
> Aimee | volpair

*Note: Aimee's name and Eutopia/Deite credibility makes this 10x more likely to get a response than a cold brand outreach.*

## 11. KPIs & Metrics

### North Star Metric
**Weekly Active Users who sent at least one Volley or opened Radar**
→ Why: This captures the two most engaging behaviours. A user who does this is sticky.

### Weekly Tracking
- Downloads (iOS + Android)
- Registrations completed (onboarding finished)
- Volleys sent
- Radar sessions opened
- Active users (DAU/WAU)

### Monthly Tracking
- Free → Premium conversion rate (target: 8% conservative / 15% optimistic)
- Premium churn rate (target: <12%)
- Club listings signed
- CAC (cost per registered user from paid channels)
- LTV trend
- App Store rating (target: maintain >4.2)

### Leading vs Lagging Indicators
| Leading (predict future) | Lagging (measure outcomes) |
|--------------------------|---------------------------|
| Radar sessions/week | Premium conversions |
| Volleys per active user | Revenue MRR |
| Profile completion rate | Churn rate |
| Day 7 retention rate | LTV |
| Referral rate | App Store ranking |

### When to Pivot
- If Day 7 retention <20% after Month 2 → onboarding problem, fix before scaling ads
- If free→premium conversion <4% after Month 3 → premium value prop weak, audit features
- If CAC >£30 per paying user after Month 4 → shift budget from paid to influencer/organic
- If club listing adoption <3 clubs by Month 3 → pricing or pitch problem, revisit offer
