# SLIDE 7 — The Volpair Score  *(proprietary tech)*

**Headline:**
```
Our proprietary compatibility engine —
built entirely from court behaviour.
```

**Sub-headline:**
```
The Volpair Score (0–100) is calculated automatically from
real match history. No questionnaires. No self-reporting.
Pure behavioural data.
```

**Score dimensions:**
```
⚡  Skill compatibility    — 25 pts   (level delta: challenge, not mismatch)
🎭  Play style match       — 20 pts   (aggressive vs defensive, net vs baseline)
📅  Availability overlap   — 20 pts   (morning/evening, weekday/weekend)
📍  Location proximity     — 15 pts   (distance between home areas)
🔥  Court chemistry        — 10 pts   (times played + post-match responses)
🌐  Social proximity       — 10 pts   (shared connections & mutual players)
────────────────────────────────────
    TOTAL                  — 100 pts
```

**Closing line (italic):** `This quality of compatibility signal doesn't exist on any dating app in the world.`

**Design notes:** Right column = radar/spider chart, two overlapping plots (Player A turquoise, Player B violet).

**Before / After:**
```

│                              │  │  The moment becomes something.│
└──────────────────────────────┘  └──────────────────────────────┘
```

**Journey:**
```
1  Download. Connect booking account in 30 seconds.
2  Profile builds automatically — level, win rate, clubs, style.
3  Answer 4 quick questions: city · intent · visibility · bio.
4  Upload a photo.
5  Instantly see everyone you've played with who's on Volpair.
6  Discover compatible players via Court Picks + Radar.
7  Send a Volley → match → start a conversation.
8  24h after the next game: "Connect with anyone you played with?"
9  The loop continues. Every game = a new opportunity.
```

**Closing:**
```
Players with an active social graph on Volpair book more courts.
More games = more matches = more activity = more games booked.
The loop is self-reinforcing — and the booking platform benefits directly.
```
