# SLIDE 2 — The Problem

**Layout:** Two-column. Left: headline + body. Right: visual (abstract illustration of disconnected people or empty court post-match).

**Headline:**
```
Padel brings people together.
Nothing helps them stay connected.
```

**Body:**
```
Every week, millions of padel players share a court,
share a moment, share a post-match drink —
and then lose each other.

Booking platforms do one thing brilliantly:
get players onto courts.

But the moment the match ends, the social
layer disappears into nothing.

→  No way to reconnect with someone you
   had a great game with

→  No way to find a compatible doubles
   partner based on real play data

→  No way to turn a shared court
   into a shared life

Tinder, Bumble, Hinge?
They match on stated preferences.
A profile. A photo. A guess.

Padel booking platforms already have
the real data. It's sitting idle.

Volpair activates it.
```

**Design notes:**
- Bold turquoise arrows on the bullet points
- Right column: muted illustration — silhouettes of two players walking off court in opposite directions
- Quote-style callout box for the last line: "Volpair activates it." — white on turquoise
