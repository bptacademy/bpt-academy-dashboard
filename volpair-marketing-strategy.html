01 — Brand Positioning
Who volpair is — and who it's for
volpair

Core Message

volpair is not a dating app. It's where padel players find their people — a playing partner, a community, a night out, and sometimes something more.

The dating angle is real, but it's one outcome of a social platform built around a shared obsession. Tinder is for anyone. volpair is for your tribe.

Primary Tagline

"Every rally starts with a match."

Works on three levels: a padel rally, a romantic match, finding a playing partner. Active, sport-specific, unforgettable. No other app can say this.

volpair

10k+ followers

  • 1 Reel + 3 Stories per month

2k–10k followers
  • Free premium + £0.50 per signup via their link
  • 1 post/month + referral code in bio

  • Target: 10–20 micro influencers by Month 3
Ideal profiles
Club coaches, active tournament players, padel lifestyle accounts

Ambassador
<2k followers
  • Free premium subscription only
  • Organic posts when they feel like it
  • Target: 20–50 ambassadors

Who they are
BPT Academy coaches & students, club regulars, early match story sharers

10 — KPIs & Metrics
What we measure. When we pivot.
volpair

North Star Metric

Weekly Active Users who sent a Volley or opened Radar

This captures the two most engaging behaviours. A user who does either is sticky and likely to retain.

Weekly Tracking

  • Downloads (iOS + Android)
  • Registrations completed (onboarding finished)
  • Volleys sent
  • Radar sessions opened
  • Daily / Weekly Active Users

Monthly Tracking

  • Free → Premium conversion rate (target: 8% con / 15% opt)
  • Premium churn rate (target: <12%)
  • Club listings signed
  • CAC from paid channels
  • App Store rating (maintain >4.2)

Leading vs. Lagging Indicators

Leading (predict future)Lagging (measure outcomes)
Radar sessions/weekPremium conversions
Volleys per active userRevenue MRR
Profile completion rateChurn rate
Day 7 retentionLTV trend
Referral rateApp Store ranking

When to Pivot

  • Day 7 retention <20% after M2 → onboarding problem. Fix before scaling ads.
  • Conversion <4% after M3 → premium value prop weak. Audit features.
  • CAC >£30 after M4 → shift budget from paid to influencer/organic.
  • <3 clubs by M3 → pricing or pitch problem. Revisit offer.
