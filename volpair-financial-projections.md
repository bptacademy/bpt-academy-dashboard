## 2. Revenue Model

### 2.1 Premium Subscription
- **Price:** £19.97/month
- **Trial:** 7-day free trial
- **Features:** Unlimited volleys, radar access, advanced filters, read receipts, priority listing
- **Conversion assumption:** 8% (conservative) / 15% (optimistic) of registered users

### 2.2 Club Party Listings
- **Monthly package:** £49/month — unlimited event posts while subscribed
- **Single event:** £29/event post (one-off)
- **Launch offer:** First 3 months at £29/month to drive early adoption
- **What clubs get:** Sponsored listing visible to all volpair users in their region, with event details, photos, ticket link
- **volpair's role:** Pure advertising platform — clubs handle all operations (venue, tickets, bar, music). Zero operational risk or liability for volpair.
- **Why clubs pay:** Direct access to engaged padel players who are actively social and looking for events. More targeted than Instagram ads, cheaper than Eventbrite promoted listings.
- **Comparison:** Instagram promoted post ~£5–15/day (untargeted), Eventbrite listing + promotion ~£50–200/event, volpair = £29–49/month for always-on visibility to the exact audience

### 2.3 Platform Partnership Revenue Share
Revenue share is deducted from gross revenue during each partnership phase:
- Phase 1 (0–500 active users): OTC — 20% of net subscription revenue
- Phase 2 (500–5,000 active users): Matchi — 15% of net subscription revenue  
- Phase 3 (5,000+ active users): Playtomic — 10% of net subscription revenue
- Club listing revenue: NOT subject to revenue share (direct volpair income)

## 8. Club Party Listings — Pricing Strategy

### Why £49/month?
- Equivalent to ~1.6 Instagram promoted posts per month (untargeted audience)
- Cheaper than one Eventbrite feature listing (~£75–200)
- Targets the exact audience: padel players who are actively social
- Clubs can post unlimited events — one packed party night covers the fee many times over

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | What's included |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Single event | £29/post | One event listing, active for 30 days |
| Monthly | £49/month | Unlimited events, featured placement, club profile badge |
| Launch offer | £29/month | First 3 months (then £49/month) — drive early adoption |

### Revenue Potential

| Clubs paying | Monthly Revenue | Annual Revenue |
|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| 30 clubs | £1,470 | £17,640 |
| 80 clubs | £3,920 | £47,040 |
| 150 clubs | £7,350 | £88,200 |
| 450 clubs | £22,050 | £264,600 |

### Why Clubs Will Pay
1. **Targeted reach** — their exact audience, already on a padel app
2. **Zero effort** — post once, volpair users discover it
3. **No operational involvement from volpair** — clubs run everything, volpair is the noticeboard
4. **Network effect** — clubs that post drive their own members to download volpair, growing the platform
