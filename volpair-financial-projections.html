2. Revenue Model

2.1 Premium Subscription

  • Price: £19.97/month with 7-day free trial
  • Features: Unlimited volleys, radar access, advanced filters, read receipts, priority listing
  • Conversion: 8% of registered users (conservative) / 15% (optimistic)

2.2 Club Party Listings

  • Monthly package: £49/month — unlimited event posts
  • Single event: £29/post, active 30 days
  • Launch offer: First 3 months at £29/month
  • volpair's role: Pure advertising platform — clubs handle all operations. Zero operational risk or liability for volpair.
  • vs. alternatives: Instagram promoted post ~£5–15/day (untargeted), Eventbrite ~£50–200/event, volpair = £29–49/month always-on visibility to the exact audience

8. Club Party Listings — Pricing Strategy

volpair acts as a pure discovery platform. Clubs handle 100% of event operations — venue, tickets, bar, music. volpair provides the audience. Zero operational risk for volpair.

Pricing Tiers

TierPriceWhat's Included
Single event£29/postOne listing, active 30 days, photos + ticket link
Monthly subscription£49/monthUnlimited events, featured placement, club profile badge
Launch offer£29/monthFirst 3 months — then £49/month (drives early adoption)

Why £49/month Is the Right Price

  • ~1.6 Instagram promoted posts at £30/post (untargeted, generic audience)
  • Less than one Eventbrite featured listing (£75–200/event)
  • One packed party night at a padel club = 50–200 players × £10–30 entry = £500–6,000 revenue for the club
  • Fully targeted — every user on volpair is already a padel player
