## MODULE 1 — Tuesday 3 April
### Theme: Groundstrokes + Serve Intro
*(Beginners & Intermediate: technique reset. Advanced: glass + tactical.)*

---

### BEGINNERS

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Free rally in a reduced court (yellow marks on the middle). Players work on keeping the ball in play, slow and controlled.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand*
- **Drill:** 1 player in the back hitting forehands. Use a cone behind the player to make them recover position after each shot. Use a cone on the opposite side to guide their target direction.
- **Rally:** 2 players in the back, forehand to forehand. Goal: keep the ball in play with long rallies. Court reduced with yellow marks to slow the pace and improve precision.

*Exercise 2 — Backhand*
- **Drill:** Same structure as above but for backhand.
- **Rally:** 2 players in the back, backhand to backhand. Same reduced court setup.

**Second Part — Serve Intro + Game (25 min)**
- **Serve practice:** 1–2 players serving from each side. Coach demonstrates grip and motion. Focus on getting the ball in, not power. Rotate after 5 serves each.
- **Game 1:** 4 players all in the back. Coach feeds the ball, rally starts. Goal: reach 10 consecutive shots without missing. Communicate!
- **Game 2:** Same setup — each team has 5 lives. Lose a life when you miss. First team to 0 lives loses.
- *If time left:* Tiebreak to 10, all players starting from the back.

---

### INTERMEDIATE

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Free rally, full court. Mix of groundstrokes, moving side to side.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand*
- **Drill:** 1 player in the back hitting forehands. Cone to recover position, cone to guide target.
- **Rally:** 2 players in the back, forehand to forehand. Ball must bounce before the service line (deep and controlled).

*Exercise 2 — Backhand*
- **Drill:** Same structure as above but for backhand.
- **Rally:** 2 players in the back, backhand to backhand. Ball must bounce before the service line.

**Second Part — Serve + Game (25 min)**
- **Serve practice:** Players serve and the other returns. Rotate after 5 serves. Focus on consistent motion and placement — not just getting it in.
- **Game 1:** 2 players in the back, 2 at the net. Coach feeds ball to the back players. Rally starts after the first shot. Goal: 10–15 consecutive shots. Communicate!
- **Game 2:** Same formation. Each team has 5 lives. Lose a life on a miss. First team to 0 loses.
- *If time left:* Tiebreak to 10, starting from the back.

---

### ADVANCED

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court warm up including overhead smashes (no bandeja yet — just arms loose).

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand + Side Glass*
- **Drill:** 1 player in the back. Forehand plus immediate side glass recovery. Cone to mark recovery position.
- **Rally:** 2 players in the back. One plays down the line, the other defends using forehand off the glass.

*Exercise 2 — Backhand + Side Glass*
- **Drill:** Same structure but backhand, using the glass.
- **Rally:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Net player directs ball to the backhand. Back player aims down the line or cross; net player volleys to backhand consistently.

**Second Part — Tactical Points (25 min)**
- **Points 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Normal coach-fed points. Focus: net team controls the point — no passive volleys.
- **Points 2:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Back players must use the glass at least once per point. If they win the rally off the glass — counts double.
- *Last 10 min:* King of the court. 4 players, winners stay on. Rotate challengers.

## MODULE 2 — Friday 8 April
### Theme: Volleys + Net Game + Serve

---

### BEGINNERS

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Reduced court rally. Double-touch warm up: players must tap the ball once with the racket before hitting it over. Slows things down and improves control.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand Volley*
- **Drill:** 1 player at the net hitting forehand volleys. Cone behind to recover to the T position after each shot. Cone on the other side to guide where to aim.
- **Rally:** 1 at the net, 1 in the back. Back player hits groundstrokes; net player volleys only. Volleys must land in the reduced area (before service line).

*Exercise 2 — Backhand Volley*
- **Drill:** Same structure but backhand volley.
- **Rally:** 1 at the net, 1 in the back. Volleys only from the net player, groundstrokes from the back.

**Second Part — Serve + Game (25 min)**
- **Serve practice:** Focus on stance and toss. Rotate after 5 serves each.
- **Game 1:** 2 at the net, 2 in the back. Coach feeds to the back. No lobs allowed — only flat shots. Goal: 10–15 consecutive shots.
- **Game 2:** 4 players start in the back. Coach feeds to one side — those two go to the net. Other side plays flat from the back only (no lobs). Tiebreak to 10.
- *If time left:* Free tiebreak to 10.

---

### INTERMEDIATE

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court rally including going to the net (no overhead smash yet).

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand Volley*
- **Drill:** 1 player at the net, forehand volley. Cone for recovery position, cone for target.
- **Rally 1:** 1 at the net, 1 in the back. Back player hits groundstrokes; net player volleys only (before service line).
- **Rally 2 (progression):** Same setup but after both net players have done their volley, switch to volley-to-volley between the two net players.

*Exercise 2 — Backhand Volley*
- **Drill:** Same structure but backhand.
- **Rally 1:** 1 at the net, 1 in the back. Groundstrokes vs volleys.
- **Rally 2:** Volley-to-volley after the drill.

**Second Part — Serve + Game (25 min)**
- **Serve practice:** Return practice included — one serves, one returns. Rotate every 5 serves. Focus: returner picks a side and commits.
- **Game 1:** 2 at the net, 2 in the back. No lobs. Off-glass counts double.
- **Game 2:** 4 players in the back. Coach feeds one side — they go to the net. Other side defends flat. Tiebreak to 10.

---

### ADVANCED

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court warm up with overhead smashes included.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand Volley + Drop Shot Intro*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net. Coach feeds short balls — player decides: volley away or drop shot (soft touch just over the net). Alternate so they practise both.
- **Rally:** 1 at the net, 1 in the back. Net player mixes volleys and drop shots. Back player must read and react.

*Exercise 2 — Backhand Volley + Drop Shot*
- **Drill:** Same as above but backhand side.
- **Rally:** 1 at the net, 1 in the back. Net player uses both backhand volley and drop shot variation.

**Second Part — Tactical Points (25 min)**
- **Points 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Net team must use at least one drop shot per point. If the drop shot wins the rally — counts double.
- **Points 2:** Normal tiebreak to 7. Rule: net team cannot smash — only volleys and drop shots. Forces touch and placement over power.
- *Last 10 min:* King of the court.

## MODULE 3 — Tuesday 10 April
### Theme: Back Glass + Transition (Defense to Attack)

---

### BEGINNERS

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Rally in the middle, reduced court.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand off the Back Glass*
- **Drill:** Coach feeds a deep ball; player hits it after the back glass using a forehand. Cone to mark recovery position, cone on the other side for the target direction. Keep it easy — ball fed between the player and the middle of the court.
- **Rally:** 2 in the back. Both players aim deep and down the line. Both defend using forehand off the glass.

*Exercise 2 — Backhand off the Back Glass*
- **Drill:** Same structure but backhand. Coach feeds deep to the backhand side.
- **Rally:** 2 in the back. Both aim deep and down the line. Both defend using backhand off the glass.

**Second Part — Transition + Game (25 min)**
- **Tactical 1:** 4 in the back. Coach feeds deep — players return off the glass and immediately move up to the net (offensive transition). Point continues from the net.
- **Tactical 2:** Same setup. If a player defends off the glass and wins the rally — counts double.
- *If time left:* King of the court.

---

### INTERMEDIATE

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court rally, going to the net included (no overhead).

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand off the Back Glass*
- **Drill:** Same as Beginners — forehand off the glass, cone recovery + target.
- **Rally 1:** 2 in the back. Aim down the line, deep. Defend using forehand off glass.
- **Rally 2 (progression):** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Net player directs deep to the back; back player defends off the glass and tries to pass the net player.

*Exercise 2 — Backhand off the Back Glass*
- **Drill:** Same structure, backhand side.
- **Rally 1:** 2 in the back. Down the line, deep. Backhand off glass.
- **Rally 2:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Back player defends backhand off glass.

**Second Part — Transition + Game (25 min)**
- **Tactical 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Coach feeds deep to the back. Back players defend off glass, then transition to the net. Net players let the ball come to them — the point starts once everyone is in position.
- **Tactical 2:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Lob + transition. Back players start with a lob, follow it up to the net. Net players must let the lob bounce — defensive transition to the back — then build from there.
- *If time left:* King of the court.

---

### ADVANCED

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court warm up with overheads.

**First Part — Drill + Rulo Intro (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Side Glass + Back Glass (Mixed)*
- **Drill:** Coach feeds varied balls — some to the side glass, some to the back glass. Player must read the ball and adapt their position accordingly.
- **Rally:** 2 in the back. Random mix of glass shots — player reads and defends.

*Exercise 2 — Rulo Intro*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net. Coach feeds a low ball — player practises the rulo (wrist-heavy topspin smash, angled towards the side glass). Focus on technique: grip, swing path, follow-through. Go slow — this is a technique drill.
- **Rally:** 1 in the back plays a lob; 1 at the net attempts the rulo. Back player practices the defensive position after the rulo bounces off the glass.

**Second Part — Tactical Points (25 min)**
- **Points 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Back team must use the glass at least once per point. Net team practices rulo when a lob presents itself. No pressure to go for it — it's a learning environment.
- **Points 2:** Tiebreak to 7. Rule: any rally that ends with a rulo winner — counts double. Encourages the attempt.
- *Last 10 min:* King of the court.

## MODULE 4 — Friday 15 April
### Theme: Lob + Bandeja/Víbora

---

### BEGINNERS

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Reduced court rally. Players focus on height and depth of shots.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Lob*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net hitting lobs. Cone to recover after each shot. Cone/zone on the other side to aim for (deep, behind the service line).
- **Rally:** 2 in the back. Lob to lob. Keep the ball high and deep. Goal: consistent depth, not speed.

*Exercise 2 — Bandeja/Víbora (intro)*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net. Coach feeds a lob — player practises the bandeja (or víbora for more advanced beginners). Cone for recovery, cone for target direction.
- **Rally:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Back player hits only low groundstrokes; net player volleys only. Keep volleys slow and in control.

**Second Part — Serve + Game (25 min)**
- **Serve practice:** 5 serves each, rotate. Keep the focus on toss and contact point — simple and clean.
- **Tactical 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Coach feeds a lob to the net players — they start the point with a bandeja or víbora.
- **Tactical 2:** Coach feeds to the back players — they start with a lob. Net players respond with a bandeja or víbora. Point continues.
- *If time left:* Tiebreak to 10, all starting from the back.

---

### INTERMEDIATE

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court rally. Include going to the net with overhead.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Lob*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net hitting lobs. Cone for recovery, cone/zone for target (behind service line).
- **Rally 1:** 2 in the back, lob to lob — high and deep.
- **Rally 2 (progression):** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Back player hits low groundstrokes; net player volleys only, keeping them low (before service line).

*Exercise 2 — Bandeja/Víbora*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net. Coach feeds lob — player executes bandeja or víbora. Cone for recovery position.
- **Rally:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Back player: one lob, two low shots. Net player: one víbora, two volleys. Structured rhythm — builds pattern recognition.

**Second Part — Serve + Game (25 min)**
- **Serve + return practice:** One serves, one returns. Returner picks a target side. Rotate every 5 serves.
- **Tactical 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Coach feeds lob to net team — point starts with bandeja/víbora.
- **Tactical 2:** Back team starts with a lob, net team responds with bandeja/víbora. Full rally continues.
- **Tactical 3:** Back player varies — lobs, low shots, glass. Net team must read and react. Back team cannot come to the net.

---

### ADVANCED

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court with overheads.

**First Part — Rulo + Drop Shot + Bandeja/Víbora (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Bandeja/Víbora vs Lob*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net. Coach alternates lobs to different positions. Player moves and plays bandeja or víbora depending on position and height.
- **Rally:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Back: one lob, two low. Net: one víbora, two volleys.

*Exercise 2 — Rulo + Drop Shot (net weapon choices)*
- **Drill:** Coach feeds: high ball → rulo. Low ball → drop shot. Player must read and decide quickly.
- **Rally:** 1 in the back hits varied shots. Net player uses all weapons — bandeja, víbora, rulo (on high balls), drop shot (on low balls). Back player practises defensive positioning after each.

**Second Part — Tactical Points (25 min)**
- **Points 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Net team uses every tool in their kit — no restrictions. Back team: glass counts double if they win the rally.
- **Points 2:** Tiebreak to 7. Rule: net team must earn their position — they can only stay at the net if they won the previous rally. If they lose a rally, they rotate back.
- *Last 10 min:* King of the court.

## MODULE 6 — Friday 22 April
### Theme: Transition — Defense to Attack

---

### BEGINNERS

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Reduced court rally. Focus on footwork — recover position after every shot.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Transition intro*
- **Drill:** 1 in the back. Coach feeds deep — player defends off the glass and moves to the net (transition). Stop and reset each time. Practise the movement pattern, not the point.
- **Rally:** 2 in the back. One player defends off glass, the other moves to support. They try to finish the point at the net.

*Exercise 2 — Serve*
- **Serve practice:** Focus on disguising the direction slightly. Where are you aiming? Pick left or right each time. 5 serves each, rotate.

**Second Part — Tactical Game (25 min)**
- **Tactical 1:** 4 in the back. Coach feeds deep — players return off glass and transition to net. Point continues from the net. Focus: move together as a team.
- **Tactical 2:** Same but with this rule — the team that transitions to the net first gets a point bonus (if they win the rally). Encourages proactive movement.
- *If time left:* Tiebreak to 10, starting from the back.

---

### INTERMEDIATE

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court rally, going to the net included.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Transition (Defense-Attack)*
- **Drill:** 1 in the back. Coach feeds deep — player returns off the glass and immediately moves to the net. Repeat until the movement is automatic.
- **Rally 1:** 2 in the back. One defends off glass, both transition. Point continues.
- **Rally 2:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Back players start with a lob, then transition. Net players let the lob bounce — defensive transition to the back — then recover and build.

*Exercise 2 — Serve + Return*
- Serve and return. Server picks a direction. Returner plays it back into a target zone. Rotate every 5 serves.

**Second Part — Tactical Game (25 min)**
- **Tactical 1:** 4 in the back. Coach feeds deep. Players return off glass and transition. If they win the rally from the net — counts double.
- **Tactical 2:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Back team plays lob + transition. Net team defends and tries to recover. First to 5 points.
- *If time left:* King of the court.

---

### ADVANCED

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court with overheads.

**First Part — Net Dominance (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Attacking the net*
- **Drill:** 2 players at the net. Coach feeds random balls — high, low, middle. They must communicate fast and play the right shot: volley, bandeja, víbora, rulo, or drop shot.
- **Rally:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Net team plays aggressively — look for the put-away. Back team plays defensively — glass, lobs, variation.

*Exercise 2 — Down the line*
- **Points:** 2 in the net vs 1 in the back. The defender plays two shots to the same player each time — alternating direction (down the line, then cross). Forces the defender to control direction under pressure.

**Second Part — Full Tactical Points (25 min)**
- **Points 1:** Tiebreak to 7. Rule: net team must win from the net — no back-court play once they're at the net. If they get pushed back, they lose a life.
- **Points 2:** Tiebreak to 7. Free play. All shots allowed. Focus on reading the game — when to attack, when to defend, when to use the glass.
- *Last 10 min:* King of the court.

## MODULE 7 — Tuesday 24 April
### Theme: Mixed Game — All Skills Combined

---

### BEGINNERS

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Double touch rally (tap the ball once before hitting) in a reduced court. Slows things down, builds control.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Forehand + Backhand Volley (random)*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net. Coach feeds randomly — forehand then backhand, no warning. Player must read and react.
- **Rally:** 1 at the net, 1 in the back. Net player mixes volleys. Back player aims low, trying to make the volley harder.

*Exercise 2 — Lob + Volley combo*
- **Drill:** 1 at the net hitting lobs. Cone for recovery, cone for target (deep).
- **Rally:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Back player: two low, one lob. Net player: two volleys, one bandeja. Structured rhythm.

**Second Part — Serve + Points (25 min)**
- **Serve practice:** Quick round — 3 serves each, focus on getting it in with intention.
- **Game 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Normal coach-fed points. Play naturally — no restrictions.
- **Game 2:** Tiebreak to 10. All start from the back. If a player defends off the glass and wins the rally — counts double.

---

### INTERMEDIATE

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court rally, including net approach.

**First Part — Drill + Rally (25 min)**

*Exercise 1 — Glass + Volley*
- **Drill:** 1 in the back. Coach feeds to the back glass — player defends and aims deep.
- **Rally:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Back player uses glass when possible. Net player volleys and varies placement.

*Exercise 2 — Lob + Bandeja + Variation*
- **Rally:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Back player varies freely — lob, glass, low groundstroke. Net player reads and responds. Back player cannot come to the net.

**Second Part — Serve + Points (25 min)**
- **Serve + return:** Server aims to a zone. Returner picks a target. Rotate every 5.
- **Game 1:** 2 in the back, 2 at the net. Back team: glass off the back glass counts double. Net team: drop shots count double. Creates tactical decision-making.
- **Game 2:** King of the court. Tiebreaks to 10. Losers rotate out.

---

### ADVANCED

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court with overheads and overhead smashes.

**First Part — Rulo + Drop Shot + Tactical (25 min)**

*Exercise 1:*
- **Drill:** Coach feeds 3 balls in sequence — one lob (rulo), one low (drop shot), one medium (bandeja or víbora). Player must read and execute quickly. No hesitation.
- **Rally:** 1 in the back, 1 at the net. Net player uses all net weapons freely. Back player mixes defence.

*Exercise 2 — Down the line + cross-court combination*
- **Points:** 2 at the net vs 1 in the back. The back player must alternate direction every two shots. The net pair must communicate and cover both sides.

**Second Part — Tactical Points (25 min)**
- **Points 1:** Tiebreak to 7. Rule: every rally must involve at least one glass shot from the back team. If the net team wins without ever facing a glass ball, the back team loses a bonus point.
- **Points 2:** Free tiebreak to 7. Full tactical — no rules. Focus on reading the game and making smart decisions together.
- *Last 10 min:* King of the court. Winners stay on.

## MODULE 8 — Friday 29 April
### Theme: Match Play + King of the Court (Season Review)

---

### BEGINNERS

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Free rally, full court. Players choose their own warm-up structure — forehands, volleys, lobs.

**First Part — Quick Drill Review (15 min)**

Coach picks 2 drills from the month based on what the group still needs work on. Suggestions:
- Forehand or backhand off the back glass (if transition wasn't clean)
- Forehand/backhand volley (if net play was weak)
- Lob to lob rally (if height and depth need more work)

**Second Part — Match Play (35 min)**
- **Serve practice:** 3 serves each, quick round. Remind them: same motion, pick a side.
- **Match play:** Tiebreaks to 10. Coach feeds the first ball. Players rotate every tiebreak. Pairs change after each round.
- **King of the court (last 15 min):** Winners stay on. Challengers in. Everyone competes.

---

### INTERMEDIATE

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court rally, full warm up.

**First Part — Quick Drill Review (15 min)**

Coach picks based on group needs. Suggestions:
- Forehand/backhand off glass + transition
- Volley sequence (forehand then backhand, random)
- Lob + bandeja rally

**Second Part — Match Play (35 min)**
- **Serve + return:** Quick round — 3 serves each. Focus on placement.
- **Match play:** Tiebreaks to 10. Rule: glass counts double when winning a rally. Adds a tactical layer to the match.
- **King of the court (last 15 min):** Tiebreaks to 7. Winners stay on.

---

### ADVANCED

**Warm Up (10 min)**
- Full court with overheads and smashes.

**First Part — Quick Tactical Review (15 min)**

Coach picks 1–2 focused situations:
- Rulo execution under pressure (feed, go)
- Drop shot + recovery (drop shot then move back to ready position)
- Transition from glass to net

**Second Part — Match Play (35 min)**
- **Points:** Tiebreaks to 7. Free play — all shots, no rules. Full tactical.
- Optional rule for the final tiebreak: any rally won with a rulo or drop shot counts double. Ends the month on a high-skill note.
- **King of the court (last 15 min):** All players compete. Winners stay on.
